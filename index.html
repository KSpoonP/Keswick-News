About Keswick 2023

Keswick Christian shhool prides itself on getting this far and acheiving greatness in adding Jesus Christ into children from a young age causing them to not fear, but accept Jesus Christ and know there is so much he has in store for them. The 2023 - 2024 theme for Keswick Christian is Fan the Flame where children learn about friendships and so much more about Jesus' never ending love.
